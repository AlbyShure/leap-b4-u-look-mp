About the project

So leap b4 u look is a little project for me right now. However, I'm still in school, so I dont have a lot of free time, but hopefully soon I'll be able to start making some Indie Electronica, Lo-fi jazz, reggae, heck maybe even some alternative or indie rock. This website is for you guys to enjoy some of my personal picks. Below are some types of music that I enjoy and would maybe like to make in the future (probably not though). I'm a pretty chill dude but I get a lot of energy when I'm around people, (I'm social), but I still like chill music and hopefully I'll be able to give you guys some of that stuff when I get the chance. Like I said though, school is a pain and it takes up every waking moment. (DISCLAIMER: I did not make any of these songs. All rights to their respective owners. These songs are merely examples of types of music I enjoy and would maybe like to produce sometime in the future.)

About moi

I was born in Boston, which is irrelevant to the fact that I like music. I've been playing acoustic guitar for almost four years now and electric for about 2. For acoustic, I enjoy playing all types of music, from alternative rock to indie pop. On electric, I enjoy playing jazz and rock. I find heavy metal really fun to play but it's way to agressive for me to listen to. My musical taste is (not to boast) extremely diverse and some of my favorite songs are ecclectic. If there is a genre of music out there I have not listened to, I'll try it. My email is allanh03@yahoo.com That's about it for basic info, so I'll let you enjoy this music. Thanks.
